About Us

We are a group of 4 students from University of Westminster, trying to develop a mobile web application to promote products included in the book retailer’s portfolio in close proximity to them, events, talks, reading sessions, interest groups meetings, children parties etc. This is a group project representing the coursework for "Mobile User Experience" module.

Our Mission

Our aim is to familiarise customers with the products the book retailers offers in stores near to them, encourage customers to visit the stores and share their experiences with others.

Why Us?

We encourage a loyalty initiative that encourages customers to advocate and engage more with the products included in the book retailers portfolio on a repeat basis.
