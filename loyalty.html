Frequently asked questions:

How many loyalty points do I get for an event booking?

We give 1 loyalty point for every booking you make! or QR code scans you make. When you have 3 points you will recieve a voucher. On claiming the voucher your points will reset.

How do I redeem my loyalty points?

When you have collected 3 loyalty points (they are going to be shown above) you can contact one of the team members who is gonna send you the money back for your forth booking.

I have just booked a ticket for an event. How can I check that my loyalty points have been redeemed on this order?

It will be displayed on the email confirmation that is sent out automatically when the booking is made as well as on this page.

Do loyalty points expire?

Yes, you must redeem your points within 12 months or they expire.
